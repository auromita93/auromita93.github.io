Bioenergy & Bioproducts
Let's look at our system holistically, where everything is linking
Collaboration and teamwork are the keys to systems thinking
For our ecosystem deserves to be preserved and protected
Sustainability is a concept that will no longer be neglected
Instead of using fossil fuels, emitting greenhouse gas
Why don't we turn to plants for energy, in the form of biomass?
Nonrenewable sources increase our air pollution
Algae, waste, and sugarcane are the new energy solutions!
As we transition into energy that will be renewable
We need products from material that are green and reusable
From plastics made with sugar to plant oils to make soap
These bioproducts of the future give our economy and environment hope
With climate change becoming a threat, each day at a higher degree
Let's start creating bioproducts and producing bioenergy!
